My Work

Agapay's Project Elevate

Elevate is a web portal application with three portals: an Admin, Business, and Customer. Admins would have have access to CRUD operations to manage their customers (business users). Business users would also have some CRUD operations to manage their direct customers and manage their subscription/benefit packages. The customer would be able to view their current subscriptions and the system would handle transactions automatically. I took the role of lead UI/UX designer during our design phase and later switched over to Front end developer during the development stage.
